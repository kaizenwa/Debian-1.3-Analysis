Support and Thanks

17.1 Support

Unfortunately, I do not have time to answer all the questions sent to me by WN users. I recommend that you subscribe to the mailing list of WN maintainers and ask your questions there. It is read by many knowledgable WN users (including me).

To subscribe with the address you're sending mail from, send a message to 'majordomo@cyberwerks.com', containing the line

subscribe wn-maint your.email.address

The people reading this list seem to be very helpful.
