CNIDR's Stateful Z39.50 Gateway

This is an experimental stateful Z39.50 gateway.
When you click the "Connect to Service" button, you will establish a stateful Z39.50 connection to the selected service. You may then interact with that service. If no input is received for five minutes, your session will expire and you will need to return to this page to log in again. If you experience long delays in receiving a search response, you may need to log in again.
