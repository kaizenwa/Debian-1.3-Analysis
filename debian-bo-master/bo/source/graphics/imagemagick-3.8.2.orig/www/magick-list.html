The ImageMagick mailing list is a low noise and subject oriented mailing list. The subject is the discussion of ImageMagick software and its use. Although the list is unmoderated, do not post off-topic messages to the list. Off-topic messages will result in the offender being silently removed from the list and prevented from rejoining.

Subscribe to the list

Email the following text 
    subscribe magick
in the body of a message to majordomo@wizards.dupont.com (don't place it in the Subject: field!).

Unsubscribe from the list

Email the following text 
    unsubscribe magick
in the body of a message to majordomo@wizards.dupont.com (don't place it in the Subject: field!)
