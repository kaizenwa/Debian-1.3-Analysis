Introduction

(Mirror Site Information is current as of July 31, 1996)

The following is a listing of mirror sites - both WWW and FTP - that are hosting the Linux Gazette. My sincerest thanks to all the folks what have graciously offered their services and made this possible.

I've included a link and URL for each site together with the e-mail address of a contact person. If you are having difficulties accessing a particular site, please send mail to the appropriate contact person. If a link is no longer functional, please drop me a note directly.

If you'd like to mirror the Linux Gazette all you need to do is drop a quick note with the subject line reading something like "Subject: Mirror Site". Please include with this:

  • The URL for the web or ftp site
  • The name and e-mail address of the contact person
  • And, optionally, your geographical location (in case I can't guess it from the domain name... :-)

You can reach me at: John M. Fisk <fiskjm@ctrvax.vanderbilt.edu>

Also, please forgive me if you sent me information about your mirror site and it doesn't yet apprear on this list. I have tried to validate each URL before including it and some of the URL's sent just frankly didn't point to anything :-( Also, mail comes in at a fairly steady pace and I do occasionally misplace something. If your site is missing, please send me the URL once again. Thanks!

Also, just to clarify a small point: the designation of "Official Site" merely means that I send the latest files directly to these sites or that I have a guest account on that site and can update pages directly. Note that a tar+gzip'd version of the Linux Gazette is available via anonymous ftp at the sites listed below. The naming designations will give you a clue as to which file you might want:

  • LinuxGazette_xxx.tar.gz files include ALL documents from the first issue of the LG in July, 1995 to the most recent.
  • lg_issuexx.tar.gz files include ONLY the most recent set of documents for that issue, as well as any modifications to previous documents.

Enjoy!
