Scrolling

  

In all text windows, a scrollbar is controllable by any mouse button to move the text vertically in the current window. A click inside the bottom or top part of the scroll area moves the text to the next or previous page.

In every text window, an horizontal scroll may be handled with the left mouse button click on the last visible character at right or first visible character at left.
